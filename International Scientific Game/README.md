
International Scientific Game Bordeaux 2025

Project Introduction:

    This project is the culmination of many days of trials and failures by our team, undertaken within the framework of the International Scientific Game Bordeaux 2025. It represents our effort to design an innovative and engaging game, utilizing only two classic 52-card decks.

Core Concept:

    Our objective was to create a unique game that maximizes the potential of a standard 52-card deck. We focused on developing game mechanics that are simple to grasp yet deeply strategic, encouraging logical thinking and adaptability from players. This game stands as a testament to the idea that creativity does not always require complex resources.


How to Play:

    To understand the gameplay of our game in full detail, please watch our game tutorial video. This video will illustrate the rules, setup, and main flow of play, demonstrating how to enjoy the game with just two classic 52-card decks.

Development & Iteration:

    The development process for this game has been a continuous journey of learning, involving numerous cycles of design, testing, and refinement. Every "trial and failure" served as a valuable lesson, helping us to fine-tune the mechanics and enhance the player experience.

Key Features:

    Minimal resource requirement (2 standard 52-card decks).
    Easy-to-learn, challenging-to-master gameplay.
    Encourages strategic thinking and quick decision-making.
